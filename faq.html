Frequently Asked Questions at HZ

Below you can find answers to some of the questiones that students usually ask at HZ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  1. Go to HZ print portal and log in with your HZ account.
  2. Upload your file.
  3. Tap your HZ student card on the printer, select and print.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

  1. Log in with your HZ account.
  2. Scan your document at the printer.
  3. Confirm your HZ email and press start.

How can you book a project space?

To request a room, visit the Service Desk/Reception at the campus, or go to HZ Service/IT Portal on HZ website.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

  • Vlissingen: Parking places are available around HZ and near Prins Hendrikweg (opposite to main entrance).
  • Middelburg: Use the Kousteensedijjk car park (8-minute walk from the campus).
