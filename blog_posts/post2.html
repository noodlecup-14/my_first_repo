My Personal SWOT Analysis

My strength is that I love working in a team, and I always try my best to complete my work. I’m highly self-motivated and enjoy researching to deepen my understanding. However, I usually find it hard to work effectively under pressure, and as someone without a background in ICT, I’m aware that I have a long and challenging road ahead of me. Despite this, I see many exciting opportunities to meet new people and fellow classmates who can broaden my perspective and support my learning journey. At the same time, I acknowledge potential threats such as balancing with personal responsibilities, as I already have a family of my own. I also need to deal with pressure and self-doubt, which might affect my pace. Being aware of these factors helps me prepare and stay focused on my goals.
