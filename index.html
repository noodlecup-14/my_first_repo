Why I chose ICT?

I am a curious person, and to me, technology feels like magic as I am amazed at how people can build state-of-the-art devices that we are using every day from just 0 and 1.
That is why I am motivated to dive deep into this field to unveil the logic and creativity of technology.
I am excited to discover the many paths within ICT, from web development to data science or software engineering.
This study program fits me because I’m a self-driven learner. I am not afraid to start from zero, as long as I have a clear goal and the right learning resources.

I truly believe that passion often grows as you improve at something, not before.
As Cal Newport says in So Good They Can’t Ignore You: “Working right trumps finding the right work.”
That is exactly how I approach ICT: I don’t expect to love it overnight, but I am excited to build skills and discover what parts of this field inspire me as I grow.

My plan for the future?

  1. Learn more about computer science basics
  2. Use free online resources like CS50 and boot.dev to support my learning.
  3. Connect with classmates and teachers to build a support network.
  4. Find a specialization during the four years that suits me best.
  5. Read more books to expand my thinking and inspiration.

This is me! •⩊•
